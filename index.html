Welcome to the Museum of Game Design!

The Museum of Game Design is dedicated to preserving and showcasing the history and culture of video games, featuring interactive exhibits and educational programs.

Who We Are?

We’re nerds, just like you, who put this museum together to showcase the progression of the game industry - this includes everything from arcade, to tabletop, to modern video games, and VR. We’re open everyday except Sundays and Mondays.
