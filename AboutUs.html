Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate gamers and industry professionals, the Game Design Museum has grown from a grassroots initiative to a prominent institution dedicated to exploring the rich history and exciting future of game design. Since our inception, we have curated an extensive collection that reflects the ever-evolving landscape of digital entertainment.

Educational Intiatives

Engage with our educational programs designed to inspire the next generation of game designers. From workshops on coding basics to discussions with industry professionals, the Museum of Game Design is committed to nurturing creativity and curiosity in the world of game development.

Interactive Experiences

Immerse yourself in our hands-on exhibits, where visitors can play iconic games, experience virtual reality showcases, and even try their hand at designing their own levels. Our commitment to interactivity ensures that every visit is an engaging and memorable experience.
